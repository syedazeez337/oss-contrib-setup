# Playbook: maintainer-response

> This file is seeded from oss-contrib-setup and evolves via /evolve-playbooks
> based on real PR outcomes logged in ~/.claude/outcomes/outcomes.log

## Strategies
- Respond to review comments within 24 hours — maintainers lose momentum after that
- Never argue about style preferences — do it their way and move on
- If a requested change is large or unclear, ask one specific clarifying question before implementing
- If PR stalls 2 weeks after your last update — ping once politely. No response after another week → close and move on
- Mark each review comment as resolved after addressing it
- After pushing fixes, add a comment: "Addressed: [summary of what changed]"

## Common Mistakes
- Letting a PR sit unresponded for >3 days
- Pushing a force-push after review starts without warning
- Asking maintainers to review immediately after pushing — give them 24–48 hours
- Responding to each comment individually instead of batching responses
